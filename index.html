/ 02 — the origin

built by people who got tired of being talked down to.

Pulp started in a Brooklyn apartment between three friends, four blenders, and one shared frustration: every "wellness" drink on the shelf was either a glorified juice or a $9 lecture in a beige can.

So we made what we wanted. Real adaptogens, properly dosed. Real flavors, not "earthy notes." And bottles that look like something you'd actually post.
